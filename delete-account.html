Delete Account

You can permanently delete all game data associated with your account directly from within the game.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Settings menu.
  2. Select Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your choice.

What happens after deletion?

  • Your game progress, achievements, statistics, leagues, tournaments, inventory, and all other gameplay-related data will be permanently deleted.
  • This action cannot be undone.
  • Your current game account will no longer contain any gameplay data.
  • If you restart the game, a new game account will be created automatically.
  • If you do not want a new game account to be created, do not restart or reopen the game after the deletion process has completed.

Need help?

If you experience any issues with account deletion, please contact us using the contact information provided on our Google Play Store listing.
