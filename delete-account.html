Delete Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated game data directly from within the game.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Settings menu.
  2. Select Delete Profile.
  3. Confirm your choice.

What happens after deletion?

  • Your game progress and profile data will be permanently deleted.
  • This action cannot be undone.
  • Do not reopen the game if you want the deletion process to continue.
  • The deletion process may take several days to complete.
  • During this period you may be unable to create a new account or access the game.
  • After the deletion process is complete, you can launch the game again and create a new account.

Reset Game Progress

You can also reset your game progress without deleting your account.

How to reset your game progress

  1. Open the Settings menu.
  2. Select Reset Progress.
  3. Confirm your choice.

What happens after resetting your progress?

  • Your game progress, achievements, statistics, rankings, and other gameplay-related data will be permanently reset.
  • Your account will remain active.
  • Your device identifier and account information will be retained so you can continue playing with the same account.
  • This action cannot be undone.

Need help?

If you experience any issues with account deletion or resetting your game progress, please contact us using the contact information provided on our Google Play store listing.
