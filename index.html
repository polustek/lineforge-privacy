Privacy Policy

LineForge ("the Game") is developed and published by PolusTek. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, stored, shared, and protected when you use LineForge.

Information We Collect

The Game may collect limited information required to provide gameplay and online services, including:

  • Device identifiers used to create and manage your game account.
  • Your chosen in-game player name.
  • Game progress and cloud save data.
  • Leaderboard rankings and multiplayer statistics.
  • Basic diagnostic information required for service operation.

The Game does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, payment card information, or precise location.

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Create and maintain your game account.
  • Synchronize cloud save data across devices.
  • Provide online multiplayer functionality.
  • Display leaderboards and rankings.
  • Prevent cheating, abuse, and unauthorized activity.
  • Improve game stability, performance, and user experience.

Some features of LineForge are available offline. Features such as multiplayer gameplay, cloud saves, and leaderboards require an active internet connection.
