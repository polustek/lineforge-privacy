Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

LineForge ("the Game") is developed and published by PolusTek. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information while using LineForge.

Information We Collect

The Game may collect limited information required for gameplay and online features, including:

  • Device identifiers used to create and manage your game account.
  • Your chosen in-game player name.
  • Game progress and cloud save data.
  • Leaderboard rankings and multiplayer statistics.
  • Basic diagnostic information required for service operation.

The Game does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, payment card information, or precise location.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and maintain your game account.
  • Synchronize cloud save data.
  • Provide multiplayer functionality.
  • Display leaderboards.
  • Prevent cheating and abuse.
  • Improve gameplay stability and performance.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses trusted third-party services, including:

  • PlayFab (account management, cloud data, leaderboards)
  • Photon PUN (online multiplayer matchmaking)
  • LevelPlay / ironSource (advertising services)
  • Unity Technologies (game engine)

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising

The Game may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may collect limited device information to provide and measure advertisements.

Offline Play

Some features of LineForge can be played offline. Online features such as multiplayer, cloud saves, and leaderboards require an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended to collect personal information from children knowingly. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to help protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including requesting access, correction, or deletion where applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes become effective immediately after being published on this page.
